# Pilot Merchant List — Venmo Group Pay Later

*Scaffolded. Named candidates + category rationale + pilot brief. Full outreach sequence and pilot LOI template available on request.*

## Target Categories (Y1)

| Category | Why it fits | Target merchants |
|---|---|---|
| **Ticketing** | Group baskets structurally; discretionary spend; 18–35 skew | Ticketmaster, Dice, AXS, SeatGeek, Eventbrite |
| **Travel** | Large basket, inherently group (roommates, couples, families); BNPL lift on AOV is high | Airbnb, Booking.com, Hopper, Vrbo |
| **Group gifting** | Mid-basket, gift-giving moments are social-feed-appropriate | Amazon, Apple, Crate & Barrel, Etsy |
| **Restaurants / experiences** | High-frequency small group baskets; proof point for Venmo-native shared commerce | OpenTable (dining experiences), Tock (tasting menus) |

## Pilot Brief (template)

- **Term:** 12 months, with 6-month renewal option.
- **Scope:** Group Pay Later + Solo Pay in 4 at Venmo checkout; no change to merchant fee band vs PPL.
- **Data:** Shared attribution on conversion lift; shared delinquency on BNPL-funded transactions; 90-day NPS on Group Pay Later purchasers.
- **Marketing:** Co-marketing moment at launch, optional "Group Pay Later at [merchant]" badge in Venmo app.
- **Out:** No exclusive; merchant retains freedom to offer other BNPL (Affirm, Klarna) in parallel.
